ADC configuration

The ADC channels have a fixed mapping:

Channel MCU Pin Arduino pin on board
AIN0 P0.26 – (exposed, by no Arduino UNO pin)
AIN1 P0.27 – (exposed, by no Arduino UNO pin)
AIN2 P0.01 A0
AIN3 P0.02 A1
AIN4 P0.03 A2
AIN5 P0.04 A3
AIN6 P0.05 A4
AIN7 P0.06 A5

Expose those on Arduino pins A0 to A5
